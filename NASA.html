To: director@nasa.gov

From: jamesj@nasa.gov

Dear All,

Today marks the anniversary of the moon landing. To commemorate this moment, we now move a piece of history into this exhibit that we have had in storage since that day. The LTX-71 concealable mike will now be displayed openly for the public to see the mike that broadcasted the words, "That's one small step for man, one giant leap for mankind."

Making small steps,

James Johnson
